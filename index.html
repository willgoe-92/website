Quick Bio

Kansan, GISP, Chief's Fan and World's Greatest Uncle.

Currently a GIS Solutions Engineer for geocgi, I work primarily on the Marine Corps Critical Asset Management System (MCCAMS). I hold a Bachelor's degree in Geography from Kansas State University and am pursuing a Master's in Cybersecurity from the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. I created this website as a working demo for my mentees in the Geospatial Professional Network.

Skills

I have extensive experience deploying and configuring ArcGIS Enterprise 11.1, ArcGIS Pro, and ArcMap, along with GIS database configuration and ETL pipeline development. My technical skills include scripting in Python, JavaScript, React, HTML, CSS, and R. Additionally, I have deployed GeoServer, configured ArcGIS Image Server for imagery processing, conducted field surveying and data collection, and authored technical documentation and graphics.
